ABOUT US

Dr. John Yesudhas, Founder & CEO of Group,he is a visionary leader in modern agriculture dedicated to advancing innovation, sustainability, and precision farming. Through his diversified ventures—IGO Agritech Farms, IGO Agri Mart, IGO Nursery, Valluvam Oils, Farmers Factory, and Protein Cuts—he has created impactful solutions that empower farmers and deliver quality products to consumers. Honored with multiple national awards, Dr. Yesudhas continues to inspire the agri-industry by blending traditional wisdom with cutting-edge technology to build a sustainable future for farming.

With 10+ years of expertise, he leads iGo Group and its brands, transforming farming through precision agriculture, hydroponics, and sustainable practices. He mentors agri-entrepreneurs, providing guidance, training, and strategies for profitable and innovative agricultural ventures.

To position IGO Group as a leader in tech-driven farming and diversified enterprises, delivering precision agriculture, empowering farmers, and expanding into BPO to create jobs and build a lasting legacy.

To revolutionize agriculture through technology, sustainability, and innovation, while expanding IGO Group into new frontiers like BPO to create opportunities and empower communities.
